Homecoming Game

Homecoming Tailgate

Alumni Pavilion — next to the football field
Fri, Sep 18, 2026 · 5:15 – 6:45 PM
No charge
Hosted by the Mayfield Alumni Association
Mayfield vs Barberton · 7:00 PM kickoff

The Mayfield Alumni Association hosts a Homecoming Tailgate Party every year for alums and friends. The Class of 2011 is welcome to join. Spouses and guests are welcome. After the tailgate, head to the game or stay and enjoy the Pavilion.

Menu

  • Sausage with peppers and onions
  • Burgers & Hotdogs
  • Sauerkraut & Pizza
  • Chips, Candy & Cookies
  • Variety of sodas and water

Campus

School Tour

6116 Wilson Mills Road, Mayfield Village, OH 44143
Sat, Sep 19, 2026 · 11:00 AM
~1.5 hours · followed by lunch with teachers at 12:30 PM
Hosted by Wayne Farinacci — Alumni Association
Complimentary
Park in the back of the school near the Pavilion
Enter through Door 16 in the back

Walk the halls of Mayfield High School for the first time since graduation. Wayne Farinacci of the Alumni Association will lead the tour. Spouses and guests are welcome. Please arrive on time — the doors will be locked and late arrivals may not be able to enter.
