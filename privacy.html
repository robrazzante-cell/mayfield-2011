Your Privacy

How this site handles your data — simply put

Home/Your Privacy

What We Collect

  • Your Google name, email, and profile photo
  • Anything you add: city, employer, business info
  • Event RSVPs you submit

Who Can See It

  • Only approved classmates can view the site
  • New sign-ins require admin approval before accessing anything beyond the home page

How It's Secured

  • Sign-in is handled entirely by Google
  • Data is stored in Firebase with strict access rules
  • Only the site admin can modify or delete records

What We Don't Do

  • No ads, no tracking, no analytics
  • Your data is never sold or shared
  • We only email you for RSVPs and account approvals
